About Me

Suppose that you were sitting down at this table. The napkins are in front of you, which napkin would you take? The one on your 'left'? Or the one on your 'right'? Usually you would take the one on your left side. That is 'correct' too. But the truly correct choice is determined by whoever takes the first napkin. This, my fellow Americans, is the essence of my grand cross-country race. As President of the United States, I am pleased to invite you to the Steel Ball Run! Do you have what it takes to ride for the glory of our nation?
